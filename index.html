Most email tools track 4 signals: opens, clicks, page views, purchases. SwiftMail tracks 12+ behavioral signals — rage clicks, dead clicks, form abandonment, scroll depth, attention, frustration. Then AI explains why each visitor abandoned or converted, with a structured reason and a narrative. From any behavior, SwiftMail can trigger email, SMS, web push, mobile push, on-site popups, retargeting audiences, or a CRM task. All from $20/mo — Klaviyo charges $400+, Bloomreach $50K+/yr.

Page views, clicks, scroll depth (per section), form field focus and abandonment, rage clicks (3+ clicks within 1 second on the same element), dead clicks (clicks on non-interactive elements), runtime errors, fetch failures, time on page, session duration, exit intent, and conversions — plus standard email opens and clicks. The web SDK is ~4.4KB gzipped, async-loaded, mask-by-default for privacy. Mobile SDKs (React Native, Flutter, native iOS, native Android) coming soon.

Every session is analyzed by AI after it ends. The model reads the full event timeline — what the visitor clicked, where they hesitated, where they got stuck — and produces a structured explanation. Abandoned sessions get one of: PRICE_HESITATION, FORM_FRICTION, TRUST_CONCERN, COMPARISON_SHOPPING, DISTRACTION, TECHNICAL_FAILURE. Converted sessions: VALUE_FIT, URGENCY, SOCIAL_PROOF, CLEAR_CTA, RETURNING_INTENT, RECOMMENDATION_MATCH. You also get a narrative paragraph and suggested actions. Auto-runs hourly; instant on demand.
